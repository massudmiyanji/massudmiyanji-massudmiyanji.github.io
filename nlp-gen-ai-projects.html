NLP & GEN AI Case Studies

Dive into my collection of innovative projects in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Generative AI, where I push the boundaries of language understanding and generation. Explore my two repositories in Github and Huggingface:

  • Medical AI Chatbot: This model is a fine-tuned version of Llama-2-7b-chat-hf, trained on the AI Medical Chatbot dataset for medical question-answering tasks. It generates accurate and respectful responses, providing helpful medical advice. The model is optimized for efficiency using LoRA and quantization techniques, making it lightweight and accessible for various applications.
  • NER Tagging in German Language: This model is a fine-tuned version of XLM-RoBERTa (xlm-roberta-base) for Named Entity Recognition (NER) tasks. It has been trained on the PAN-X subset of the XTREME dataset for German Language.
  • LLM & Langchain Project Repository: This repository showcases a collection of completed projects that demonstrate the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI with LangChain, a modular framework for building language-based applications.
